Making Playlists

During this quarantine I have been listening to a lot of music and making a lot of playlists! If you use Spotify, a fun thing you can do with your friends is make a collobrative playlist and have everyone add songs. I did this with my college friends since we have been away from each other and it made me feel close to them.
