Listening to Podcasts

Because of my lack of social interaction, podcasts have been a wonderful thing to fill the time. I have been listening to some old favorites and finding some new ones. Below are my three favorites!

Schnitt Talk is hosted by Ellie Schnitt and her producer Alana through Barstool Sports. I like this because I find it funny and relatable. It touches on many aspects of being a college woman, from relationships to job opportunites.

Trillbilly Workers Party is a political podcast based in Appalachia. It is both extremely hilarious and extremely informative. I've been really into this one lately because of the election. All of the people on the podcast are very smart and make complicated issues easy to understand.

Crime Junkie is a classic true crime podcast. If you like true crime, I promise you'll like this. I enjoy this because it's a different case every epsiode, so it not a marathon to get though a story. Highly reccommend!
